---
title: Cannot Save Document
description: Fix issues saving documents in Lunda.
---

## Symptoms

- The "Save" button is unresponsive
- Error: "Save failed. Please retry."

## Causes

- Internet connection issue
- Session expired

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check connection">
    ````bash
    ping lunda.io
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh session">
    Log out, then log in again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Retry save">
    Try saving your document again.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
If issues persist, contact support.
</Callout>
