## Overview

Lunda provides a centralized space for your team's documentation. You create organized spaces, version your docs, and collaborate seamlessly. Focus on content while Lunda handles structure, search, and sharing.

<Callout kind="info">
  Lunda supports Markdown, embeds, and custom themes for professional docs.
</Callout>

Lunda streamlines documentation workflows with powerful tools.

## Getting Started

Set up your first documentation space in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus">
    Sign up at `https://lunda.com` with your email. Verify your account to unlock full features.
  </Step>
  <Step title="New Space" icon="plus">
    Click "New Space" and name it, like "Project Alpha Docs". Add a description and brand color `#3B82F6`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add First Doc" icon="file-text">
    Create a page with Markdown. Use the editor for rich content.

    <CodeGroup tabs="Markdown,HTML">
      ````markdown
      # Welcome

      This is your first doc.

      - List item
      - Another item
      ````

      ````html
      <h1>Welcome</h1>
      <p>This is your first doc.</p>
      <ul>
        <li>List item</li>
        <li>Another item</li>
      </ul>
      ````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish & Share" icon="share-2">
    Publish your space and share the link: `https://docs.lunda.com/your-space`.
  </Step>
</Steps>
